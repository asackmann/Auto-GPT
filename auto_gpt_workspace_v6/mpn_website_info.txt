The Microsoft Partner Network is a global community of partners, offerings, and resources that provide organizations with everything they need to build and deliver successful business solutions. To join, one must enroll in the Microsoft Cloud Partner Program, create a free account, share basic information about their organization and business goals, provide essential business information, confirm their company details, and validate their credentials. Once enrolled, members have access to exclusive training, support, service, and sales programs that accelerate innovation and drive growth.
