Some potential ideas for our commercial strategy to become the best data and AI partner for Microsoft in the Latin American region: 
- Develop and promote custom AI and machine learning solutions for specific industries and markets in the region
- Offer affordable pricing for our services to attract new customers and increase demand
- Establish partnerships with local universities to recruit and retain top tech talent in the region
- Leverage Microsoft's AI and machine learning programs and partnerships to gain a competitive advantage and increase our capabilities
- Set aggressive goals for percentage of tech talent performing work, accessibility and availability, daily/monthly active users and average revenue per user, and track our progress regularly.
